HOME

Indian Gamers Leauge (IGL) is a indian gaming community organized for the well fair of indian gamers. It is a platform for all type of gamers.There are various sub-commmunities for various games. We host tournaments - both competetive and free for all, available for all the members. Members can also post pictuers of their in-game records and achievement. Futher more, like minded peoples can join like-wise groups. A code of discipline must maintained between the members. Those who found to use any method cheating will be warned for the first time and next time will be banned for life time

Tournaments
